## New Driver Checklist

Welcome to **North Star Racing**. Before you take the green flag, please complete **all** items below to ensure a smooth, professional, and competitive racing experience.

---

### 1. Registration & Accounts
- [ ] **[Join the official league Discord](https://discord.gg/n4jGZ4UU5z)**
- [ ] Complete the league registration form for Cup, Trucks, or both:
    - [ ] **[Cup Series Registration Form](https://forms.gle/KyWRriyWuYWA6k9g6)**
    - [ ] **[Truck Series Registration Form](https://forms.gle/SCor6Zyuc7h57H6K9)**
- [ ] Set your Discord nickname to match your iRacing name

---

### 2. Rules & Expectations
- [ ] Read the **[Sporting Regulations](/member/02-sporting-regulations/membership/)** in full
- [ ] Review on-track conduct and incident expectations
- [ ] Understand penalty, protest, and appeal procedures
- [ ] Acknowledge broadcast and branding usage policies

> Drivers are responsible for knowing and following all league rules.

---

### 3. Car, Number & Paint
- [ ] Confirm your assigned car number:
    - [ ] **[Cup Series Roster](/cup-series/roster/)**
    - [ ] **[Truck Series Roster](/truck-series/roster/)**
- [ ] Ensure paint complies with league branding and sponsor rules
- [ ] Verify **[Trading Paints](https://www.tradingpaints.com/dashboard)** is installed and working

---

### 4. Race Weekend Preparation
- [ ] Confirm race date and start time (EST) on the schedules:
    - [ ] **[Cup Series Schedule](/cup-series/schedule/)**
    - [ ] **[Truck Series Schedule](/truck-series/schedule/)**
- [ ] Download the correct car and track content in iRacing
- [ ] Load the correct setup type (Open or Fixed)
- [ ] Test your setup in a practice session
- [ ] Verify wheel, pedals, and button bindings

---

### 5. Race Day Procedures
- [ ] Be in Discord **at least 30 minutes before race start**
- [ ] Join the iRacing session early
- [ ] Attend the driver briefing after qualifying
- [ ] Grid promptly and follow pacing instructions
- [ ] Maintain voice discipline during green-flag racing

---

### 6. In-Race Conduct
- [ ] Respect racing room and avoid reckless moves
- [ ] Hold brakes after incidents to prevent secondary contact
- [ ] Follow caution and restart procedures precisely

---
