## Competitor Release

## Sanctioning

**North Star Racing** officially sanctions the following online simulated racing series:

| Series       | Series name             |
| ------------ | ----------------------- |
| Cup Series   | North Star Cup Series   |
| Truck Series | North Star Truck Series |

### Cup Series

The **North Star Cup Series** serves as the premier division within North Star Racing. Its objective is to deliver a true-to-life simulation of the NASCAR Cup Series within the iRacing environment. The **North Star Cup Series** operates under formats, rules, and procedures closely aligned with those of the real-world NASCAR Cup Series in order to provide an authentic and consistent competitive experience for all participants.

#### Series Information

| Field                    | Description                                                                          |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Car(s)**               | NASCAR Cup Series Next Gen                                                           |
| **Race Length**          | NASCAR O'Reily's Auto Parts Series Length (Select Crown Jewel Cup full-length races) |
| **Setups**               | Open                                                                                 |
| **Stage Racing**         | 2 - based on O'Reily stages                                                          |
| **Choose Cone**          | Enabled                                                                              |
| **Restarts**             | Restart procedures and lanekeeping rules enabled                                     |
| **Tires**                | Based on the NASCAR O'Reily's Auto Parts Series Goodyear Tire Report                 |
| **Fuel**                 | 100%                                                                                 |
| **Championship Formats** | NASCAR Cup Series Championship Format                                                |
| **Race Director**        | Live - Ryan Taft                                                                     |

### Truck Series

The **North Star Truck Series** is designed to replicate the distinctive style of racing found in the NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series within the iRacing environment. Emphasizing close competition, aggressive yet respectful racecraft, and the unique dynamics of truck racing, the series delivers fast-paced events that reward adaptability and situational awareness. Fixed setups are utilized to reduce complexity and barriers to entry, fostering a more relaxed and accessible competitive environment while maintaining a strong focus on realism and on-track authenticity.

#### Series Information

| Field                    | Description                                                                                            |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Car(s)**               | NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series                                                                          |
| **Race Length**          | Stage 1 + Stage 3 of NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series Length (Select Corwn Jewel Truck full-length races) |
| **Setups**               | Fixed - iRacing default                                                                                |
| **Stage Racing**         | Normal Length - 1, Full Length - 2                                                                     |
| **Choose Cone**          | Enabled                                                                                                |
| **Restarts**             | Restart procedures and lanekeeping rules enabled                                                       |
| **Tires**                | Based on the NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series Goodyear Tire Report                                        |
| **Fuel**                 | 100%                                                                                                   |
| **Championship Formats** | NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series Championship Format                                                      |
| **Race Director**        | Live - Tyler Belanger                                                                                  |
