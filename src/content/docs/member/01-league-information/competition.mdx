## Sanctioning

**North Star Racing** officially sanctions the following online simulated racing series:

| Series       | Series name             |
| ------------ | ----------------------- |
| Cup Series   | North Star Cup Series   |
| Truck Series | North Star Truck Series |

### Cup Series

The **North Star Cup Series** serves as the premier division within North Star Racing. Its objective is to deliver a true-to-life simulation of the NASCAR Cup Series within the iRacing environment. The **North Star Cup Series** operates under formats, rules, and procedures closely aligned with those of the real-world NASCAR Cup Series in order to provide an authentic and consistent competitive experience for all participants.

#### Championship Formats

##### NASCAR Championship

This will be the standard Driver's championship with the format that NASCAR determines for the 2026 NASCAR Cup Series. This format has yet to be announced. When the format is announced, it will be fully outlined below.

##### Full Season Championship

This championship format will use standard NASCAR scoring (stage points + race points), but in a full 36-race format. The driver with the most points at the end of the 36-race season is the Full Season Champion.

##### Owner's Championship

The top two drivers from each team will have their points combined from the Full Season Championship format. The team with the most points at the end of the 36-race season is the Owner's Champion.

### Truck Series

The **North Star Truck Series** is designed to replicate the distinctive style of racing found in the NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series within the iRacing environment. Emphasizing close competition, aggressive yet respectful racecraft, and the unique dynamics of truck racing, the series delivers fast-paced events that reward adaptability and situational awareness. Fixed setups are utilized to reduce complexity and barriers to entry, fostering a more relaxed and accessible competitive environment while maintaining a strong focus on realism and on-track authenticity.

### Special Events

Races held outside of regularly scheduled leagues will be known as **Special Events**. **Special Events** will use the base Rules & Regulations, and supplemental rule sheets will be provided to participants for specific event details, rules, and regulations. Participants are expected to adhere to all base Rules & Regulations and any supplemental rules, regulations, and information provided when participating in **Special Events**.

### Crown Jewel Events

**Crown Jewel Events** refer to full-length races run during the normal course of the Cup and Truck Series' seasons. Additional rules and regulations may apply that affect start times, qualifying procedures, and field setting procedures. Participants are expected to adhere to all base Rules & Regulations and any supplemental rules, regulations, and information provided when participating in **Crown Jewel Events**.
