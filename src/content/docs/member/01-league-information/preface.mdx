## Event Sanctioning

A **North Star-sanctioned event** is a **competitive, simulated racing event hosted on a racing simulation software** which is intended to be **conducted and officiated in accordance with the rules, regulations, and procedures** in this documentation. 

## Documentation Amendments

This documentation may be amended by **North Star Racing** as deemed necessary. Special rules and regulations may be published by **North Star Racing** for specific events and distributed to participants.

## Personal Responsibility

This documentation is designed to provide for the orderly conduct of **North Star-sanctioned events**. It is the **responsibility of competitors, team owners, and community members** to address any complaint he/she may have regarding officiating via the help desk found on the Discord server. It is ultimately the responsibility of each **competitor, team owner, and community member** to ensure their conduct comply with all **North Star Racing Rules & Regulations** outlined in this documentation.
