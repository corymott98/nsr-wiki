## General Conduct

**North Star Racing** members are to conduct themselves with the highest levels of respect at all times. Members are expected to treat other members, drivers, spotters, staff, sponsors, and spectators in a fair, respectful, and courteous manner. Bullying, abusive, or threatening behavior are strictly prohibited. **North Star Administrators** has sole discretion to determine if behavior is negatively impacting other members or the league. **North Star Administrators** reserve the right to restrict communication within events or Discord server communication if members are deemed in violation of the guidelines in the Member Conduct section.

## Criticism

Constructive criticism and warranted complaints made in a respectful manner and in appropriate channels are encouraged. Comments deemed unfair or consistent negativity towards North Star Racing, iRacing.com, members, staff, or the community are strictly prohibited. ## Hateful and Discriminatory

Hateful and discriminatory language is strictly prohibited in all league communications. This includes, but is not limited to: Discord text channels, Discord Direct Messages, Discord voice channels, iRacing lobby chat, iRacing radio chat, or communications on other relevant platforms.

## Disclaimer

North Star Staff reserves the right to penalize breaches of member conduct on a case by case basis. Members have a right to appeal any penalties, unless specifically stated otherwise in the North Star Rules & Regulations.
